About the Movie

Sita Ramam is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film. It stars Dulquer Salmaan and Mrunal Thakur in the lead roles. The story is set in the 1960s and follows a soldier and his long-lost love.

Sita Ramam is a beautiful romantic drama set against the backdrop of war. It follows the journey of Lieutenant Ram and Princess Sita.

"Love is the most powerful weapon of all."
Full Synopsis

Ram, an orphan army officer, receives anonymous love letters from Sita Mahalakshmi. Their love story unfolds amidst secrets, war, and destiny.
